Banana Pancakes

Description

We made these pancakes May 29th, 2022 so I'd have another example for my website.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup All Purpose Flour
  • 8 oz milk
  • 2 ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1 Tbs Sugar
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp cream of tartar
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp tapioca starch
  • 2 Tbs vegtal ole
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1/3 cup Dolce melting chcolate wafers, roughly chopped
  • pat of butter, to fry in

Steps

  1. Mix together all dry ingredients
  2. Mix bananas, egg, oil and milk together
  3. Fold mixtures together until incorporated, batter may remain lumpy
  4. Melt butter in cast iron pan over medium heat
  5. Spoon batter into pan in 1/4 cup increments. Cook until bubbles remain on the surface after popping, and then flip.
  6. Serve warm, drowned in syrup of choice.
