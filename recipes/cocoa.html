Hot Cocoa

Description

This is my son's favorite way to start the day. He calls it hot cocoa, even though it is cold.

Ingredients

  • 1 sippy cup
  • 8 oz. of milk - 2%
  • 2 Tbs. of Hershey's Chocolate Syrup

Steps

  1. Retrieve the cup from storage.
  2. Add the milk to the cup.
  3. Measure in the required syrup.
  4. Shake the heck out of it.
  5. Serve and Enjoy!
